**AUS Logistics** is an independent business contracted with FedEx Ground to provide pickup and delivery services. Based in Austin, Texas, we have grown from a small operation to a robust fleet serving thousands of customers daily.

### Our Mission
To provide reliable, efficient, and safe delivery services while cultivating a workplace where drivers feel respected, valued, and supported.

We maintain high standards for:
* **Fleet Maintenance:** We don't cut corners on repairs.
* **Uniforms:** We ensure our team looks professional.
* **Training:** We provide paid training to ensure you are confident behind the wheel.
